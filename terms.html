Quietoak is a self-help and wellness app that helps you practice skills based on the Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) model. It is not therapy, not medical or mental-health treatment, not a diagnosis, and not a substitute for professional care. It does not establish a therapist–patient or any other professional relationship. Always seek the advice of a qualified professional with any questions about a medical or mental-health condition.

It is disclosed as AI in the app. It can be wrong, and it may produce responses that are inaccurate or not right for your situation. Do not rely on it for medical, legal, financial, or emergency decisions, and please don't treat it as a replacement for real human support or professional care.

Crisis & emergencies

Quietoak is not an emergency or crisis service. If you are in immediate danger or thinking about harming yourself, call your local emergency number now. In the US, call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or text HOME to 741741. The in-app Crisis Survival Kit provides these resources for convenience — it is always free and works offline — but it does not contact emergency services for you and is not a monitored helpline.
